Overview

Considering the intense ongoing activity on SU(N) problems, we have taken the initiative to organize an online Asian-European workshop on "SU(N) physics in condensed matter and cold atoms". The online workshop is formally hosted by Osaka University, and it will be held in the week of May 9-13, 2022.

Regarding the format, and for the convenience of all participants, we plan to have about five talks per day (9-12 am CEST, 4-7 pm JST) using Zoom.

The topics that we expect to address in this workshop include:

  • Cold atomic systems
  • Spin-orbital models
  • Moiré systems
  • Topological states of matter
  • Dynamics at zero and finite temperature
  • Numerical methods: Exact diagonalization, Quantum Monte Carlo, DMRG, etc.
