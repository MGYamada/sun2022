Ji-Yao Chen

Investigation of SU(N) spin liquids with symmetric PEPS

Quantum spin liquid state can be faithfully represented and efficiently characterized within the symmetric Projected Entangled Pair State (PEPS) framework. In this talk, I will discuss our works in recent years of constructing and characterizing both non-chiral and chiral SU(N) spin liquids with symmetric PEPS on the square lattice. Then I will present some recent results about SU(3) chiral spin liquid on the Kagome lattice using the same approach. If time permits, some further direction will be briefly mentioned.
